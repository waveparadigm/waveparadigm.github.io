History

The WaveParadigm name has been involved in over 40 games -- made in a variety of tools ranging from Cave Story modding programs to Unreal Engine 4. I've tackled platformers, puzzle games, racing games, and more.

I have written well over 25 hours of music in my life, using Pro Tools and FL Studio.

Upon entering Stevens I joined the Stevens Game Development Club; in my sophomore year, I became the organization's President. Education, competition, and comraderie are all things I strive to promote in the game dev world.
