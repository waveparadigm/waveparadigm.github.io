History

The WaveParadigm name has been involved in over 50 games -- made in a variety of tools ranging from Cave Story modding programs to Unreal Engine 4. I've tackled platformers, puzzle games, racing games, and more, both alone and in teams of varying sizes.

I have written well over 25 hours of music in my life, using Pro Tools and FL Studio.

I am a former President of the Stevens Game Development Club. I'm also a member of the International Game Developers Association and am involved in planning events local to my area. Education, competition, and comraderie are all things I strive to promote in the game dev world.
