Slime 64

2019 - Present


  • Twitter

A 3D collectathon platformer inspired by 64-bit classics. Journey around the world as members of the four Slime societies: Earth, Air, Water, and Fire. Help them come together to save their world, while collecting everything they possibly can along the way.

Slime 64 is still in active development (with a team of, at one point, 12 people!), and is on pace to be the most expansive project I've ever worked on. I'm really excited for its continued progress - follow us on Twitter!
