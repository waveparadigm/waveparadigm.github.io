Get That Bread: Privacy Policy

Thank you for playing Get That Bread! The game is an Android export of a Unity project made by a small team in 48 hours for the 2022 GMTK Game Jam.

We have not programmed this app to collect any information from its users - personally identifiable or otherwise. Any and all data created by the you (game progress, preferences, etc) is stored on your device only, and can be erased by simply clearing the app's data or uninstalling it from your device.

If you have any questions or concerns about this game, please do not hesitate to reach out to contact (at) waveparadigm.com
