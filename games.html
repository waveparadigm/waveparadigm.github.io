Spherical Saunter R/M

Two sequels to Spherical Saunter, a rolling game inspired by Kororinpa. R is very similar to the original -- you roll, jump, and collect coins to reach the coal. M is similar as well, but instead of directly rolling the ball, you tilt the course. Level design and mechanics differ for this. Summer 2017.
